### 💡 Understanding Invisible Characters

To use invisible text correctly, it is important to understand the two main types of blank characters:

**1. Zero-Width Characters (ZWJ / ZWNBSP)**
These are completely invisible markers used primarily to prevent text from breaking or to combine emojis. They take up zero visual space but register as a character in the system.

**2. Blank Braille Patterns & Hangul Fillers**
These are the heavy-duty blank characters. When a game or app completely blocks regular spaces, these specific Unicode characters bypass those restrictions, making them perfect for creating "nameless" profiles.
